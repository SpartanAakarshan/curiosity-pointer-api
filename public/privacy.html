Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

What We Collect

Curiosity Pointer collects only what is strictly necessary to operate the service:

  • Your email address and hashed password (for account creation and login)
  • The selected text you Alt+Click (sent to our server to generate an explanation)
  • A count of how many explanations you have requested (for usage tracking)

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track which websites you visit
  • We do not store your browsing history
  • We do not read any page content beyond the text you explicitly select
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising

How Your Data Is Used

Selected text is sent to our backend server, which forwards it to the Google Gemini API to generate a 2-sentence explanation. The text is not stored after the response is returned. Your email is used solely to identify your account and deliver the service.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate Curiosity Pointer:

  • Supabase — authentication and account storage
  • Google Gemini API — AI explanation generation
  • Upstash Redis — rate limiting (request counts, not content)
  • Razorpay — payment processing (Pro plan)
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We do not control how they handle data beyond what we send them.

Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored in Supabase with industry-standard encryption. Your auth token is stored locally in your browser's extension storage, which is sandboxed and inaccessible to other extensions or websites. We use HTTPS for all data in transit.

Data Retention

Your account and usage data are retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us.

Children's Privacy

Curiosity Pointer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via email. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
